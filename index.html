Who are we?

Eugene Web Developers is all about web and mobile app development in the age of ubiquitous internet. Eugene's web development community has been quietly growing and thriving. We're enthusiastically contributing to the community with cooperative learning, networking the occasionally critique, and most importantly getting to know each other.
